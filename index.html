About

Samiksha Bhattacharjee is a British author and social commentator. She wrote Legal Crime, a thriller written at age thirteen, and produces Samiksha’s State of the Debate, a series exploring global issues and campus culture. Her work advocates for free speech, classical liberal economics, and open debate. She focuses on culture, politics and economics.
