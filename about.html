About

I write about culture, economics, politics, and the uncomfortable truths that shape modern life. My work blends analysis with narrative — always grounded, always direct, always honest.

Who I Am

I’m a British author, commentator, and host of Samiksha’s State of the Debate. My fiction — including Legal Crime and the upcoming Perfected Image — explores power, identity, and the systems that govern us. My commentary focuses on the cultural and political forces reshaping the UK and beyond.

What I Do

I write weekly analysis, long‑form essays, and investigative commentary. I speak on issues ranging from higher education to free speech to the economics of modern Britain. My work aims to cut through noise and get to the truth — even when it’s uncomfortable.

My Work

My writing has appeared across commentary platforms, podcasts, and independent media. I also run a growing Substack community where I publish exclusive deep dives, behind‑the‑scenes insights, and reader‑requested content.

Why I Write

Because the world is changing fast — and too many conversations are happening in the dark. I believe in clarity, honesty, and saying the quiet part out loud. My goal is simple: to make sense of the world, and to help others do the same.
